Sochi Winter Olympics 2014

Zero Waste?

In its bid for the games, Russia pledged to follow green building standards and stage a "zero waste" event.

Critics say the games will not meet that goal, but Russia said it has used a number of alternative energy sources, including solar-powered heat radiators and boilers in the new Adler train station, and wind generators throughout the grounds of the Olympic Park.
